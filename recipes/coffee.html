Brain Juice

We all need brain juice! Best enjoyed in the early hours of your day!

This recipe is assuming you drink your coffee whith a little milk and you have a coffee maker.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Coffee
  • Water
  • Milk

Directions:

  1. Add water to your coffee maker, enough for your cup of coffee.
  2. Add filter to your coffee maker. Next pour in coffee grounds to desired strength.
  3. Hit start and let brew. When done transfer into your cheesiest mug (leave enough room for milk).
  4. Finally, add milk until golden brown and enjoy your cup of joe!
